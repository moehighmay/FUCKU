About Us

Our Mission

The Center for Foreign Languages (CFL) at AIU is dedicated to breaking down language barriers and creating employment opportunities for students. Our mission is to support zero unemployment and zero poverty through accessible language education and meaningful work experience.

Our Goals

  • Enhance foreign language skills for individuals.
  • Reduce campus unemployment by offering part-time teaching positions.
  • Provide diverse teaching methods, including online and in-class sessions.
  • Support the 8-hour rule and personal development through our programs.

Our Impact

Educational Access: Enrolled 75 students, with increased outreach through digital platforms.

Employment Creation: Generated five part-time teaching positions for students, addressing local unemployment.

Skill Development: Enhanced language proficiency and soft skills among participants.

Cultural Exchange: Promoted global perspectives and cultural understanding through our programs.

Future Plans

  • Extend Japanese classes and introduce new languages such as French and Spanish.
  • Expand to communities across Alor Setar, Kedah, and Malaysia.
  • Offer soft skills training and target rural areas for greater support.
  • Develop online courses to reach a wider audience.

Our Services

CFL operates on the AIU campus, in the LC building classrooms, with plans for online sessions. We currently offer classes in Bahasa Melayu, with future expansions in other languages.
